Enchiladas

World Famous Enchiladas!

A staple in the world of Mexican food, this dish is easy and delicious! Perfect to make year round.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Enchilada Sauce
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Protein (optional)

Steps

  1. Lay out tortillas in oil in saucepan, heat until crispy
  2. Insert cheese, protein, roll up tortillas
  3. Apply green or red enchilada sauce to top of rolled tortillas
  4. Bake for 20 minutes at 350 degrees
