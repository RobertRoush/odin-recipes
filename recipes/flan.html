Flan

Flavorful Flan!

A Mexican dessert that will delight everyone at your next dinner party!

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, milk in a mixing bowl
  2. Cover with foil and place in refrigerator for 30 minutes
  3. Add vanilla extract, add to flan mold
  4. Allow to rest overnight in refrigerator
