Tacos

Everyone's Favorite: Tacos!!

These need no introduction, arguably one of the most popular foods ever created.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (hard or soft)
  • Protein of choice
  • Cheese
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook protein, add taco seasoning
  2. Assemble taco station
  3. Heat tortillas
  4. Build tacos!
